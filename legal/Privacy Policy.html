rivers.run Privacy Policy

This privacy policy covers the following:

  • https://rivers.run
  • https://embeds.rivers.run

Data Collection:

https://rivers.run collects some information about the visitor in order to determine how the site is used. Currently, we collect: How many times you previously visited the page (to count unique visitors) The device and browser you are using (to determine what needs to be supported in the future) Features that you are using (dark mode & offline) The page you just navigated from (to determine menu structure)

https://embeds.rivers.run does not collect information, however may contain embeded sites, which rivers.run has no control over. In the future it will avoid loading those sites without a direct, informed, user action.
