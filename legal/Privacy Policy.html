Rivers.run Voice Assistant

The rivers.run voice assistant is not intended or designed for data collection, although request details may be captured in debugging logs. Rivers.run will NOT sell this information or use it for purposes other than debugging, including but not limited to advertising, tracking users, or generating aggregated statistics about it's userbase.

Rivers.run Website

The rivers.run website collects some information from visitors, including useragent (device and browser), browser capabilities, previous visits, the referring page, and if some features (like Dark Mode and Offline) are being used. Although such information is not intended to identify a single user, doing so may be possible with the information collected. Rivers.run will NOT sell this information or use it for purposes other than improving it's products and services.

Rivers.run Notifications

In order to provide it's web push notification services, rivers.run must store:

  1. When you would like to receive notifications
  2. A unique url required for sending notifications

Rivers.run does NOT sell these unique urls, or use them for purposes other than sending notifications. Information about when users would like to receive notifications is NOT sold or used for purposes other than improving rivers.run's services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately, after they are posted on this page.
