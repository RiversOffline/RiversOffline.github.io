Rivers.run Voice Assistant

Get river levels and more using your voice!

For Google Assistant:

Take a look at the rivers.run action for Google Assistant. It is able to provide water levels, relative flows, and temperatures

For Amazon Alexa:

A skill for Amazon Alexa is still under development

For Microsoft Cortona:

Microsoft requires a billable Azure account to create an action, something that is a bit of a hassle to set up.

For iOS Siri:

Interfacing with Siri requires a native app. Since Apple wants quite a bit of money every year to put an app in the app store, there are currently no plans to add support for iOS Siri.

You can however, install Google Assistant for iOS, and use our Google Assistant action.

For MacOS Siri:

I am looking into the possibility of building an application for Siri on MacOS.
