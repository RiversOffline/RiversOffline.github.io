Local Expertise

rivers.run write-ups share local expert incites into both commonly paddled rivers as well as hidden gems.

For your convenience, we are also consolidating local and regional experts into geographical and event based summary pages and lists. The lists are maintained using rivers.run tag capabilities. There are a number of tags already in place (CCCWOR, WVWOR), that work in conjunction with free form search the rivers. To take full advantage of any search result, you can hit the skill arrow to sort by difficulty. Click on any river to see the river write-up and the most current river guage information.

The more expertise, the better. If you are interested in adding your local expertise to river write-ups, or adding an events and regional summary, please see this link
